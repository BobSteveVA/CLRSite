Training

CLR provides Training to government, non-profit organizations and community members on supporting individuals with disabilities to live a productive meaningful life in the community.

Technical Assistance

CLR Associates provide technical assistance related to permanent supportive housing, transitioning youth, disability service systems, program management and program finance to a wide range of stakeholders including self-advocates, government agencies, case management organizations, service providers, and families

Consultation

CLR provides consultation and program support to government regarding program and fiscal management, system design, and quality management.
